Idaho, USA

Idaho, the "Gem State," is a haven for outdoor lovers with over 4.7 million acres of wilderness, Shoshone Falls (taller than Niagara Falls), and the Snake River Canyon. Known for producing 1/3 of the nation’s potatoes, it’s both agriculturally rich and naturally stunning. Idaho offers a low cost of living, safe communities, and the charm of all four seasons, from snowy winters to sunny summers. Boise, the state’s vibrant capital, features a growing tech industry, arts scene, and the scenic Boise River Greenbelt. Combining affordability, beauty, and a high quality of life, Idaho is an exceptional place to live.
